Important Rollover Calculation Example
When wagering on pregame straight bets, only the potential win amount is applied toward rollover—not the total risk.

Example:
If a player risks $180 to win $100, only $100 will be credited toward the rollover requirement.

This rule applies to all wagers with odds where the risk exceeds the potential payout.
